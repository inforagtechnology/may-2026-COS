    Frequently Asked Questions

    Why learn DSA with C++?

    +

    C++ is the fastest language for competitive programming, with STL providing ready-made data structures. Most top companies accept C++ in coding rounds.

    Is this course for beginners?

    +

    You should know basic C++ (variables, loops, functions) before joining. We cover C++ STL in detail at the start.

    How many LeetCode/Codeforces problems are covered?

    +

    200+ problems across all difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard) are discussed and solved during the course.

    Which companies can I target after this course?

    +

    TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Amazon, Flipkart, Zomato, Paytm, and other product-based companies.

    What is Dynamic Programming?

    +

    DP is an optimisation technique that solves complex problems by breaking them into overlapping subproblems and storing results to avoid repetition.

    How is this different from the C with C++ course?

    +

    C with C++ focuses on language fundamentals and OOP. DSA with C++ focuses entirely on problem-solving, algorithms, and interview preparation.

    Will placement assistance be provided?

    +

    Yes. Resume review, mock interviews, coding round preparation, and job referrals are included.

    Will I get a certificate?

    +

    Yes, 3 ISO-certified certifications upon completion.
