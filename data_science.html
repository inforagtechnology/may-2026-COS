    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is Data Science?

    +

    Data Science is the field of extracting knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data using statistics, programming, and machine learning.

    Who can join this course?

    +

    Students, graduates, working professionals, and anyone interested in AI/ML. Basic math and logical thinking is helpful.

    What topics are covered?

    +

    Python, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, TensorFlow, NLP, and model deployment.

    What is the course duration?

    +

    14 weeks with 108 lessons from Python basics to advanced model deployment.

    Will I get a certificate?

    +

    Yes, ISO-certified completion and project certificates from our institute.

    Is Data Science in demand in India?

    +

    Data Science is among the top 3 most in-demand jobs globally. India has a severe shortage of skilled data scientists.

    What salary can I expect?

    +

    Data Science professionals earn ₹5–10 LPA at entry level and ₹18–40 LPA with 2–3 years of experience.

    What career paths are available?

    +

    Data Analyst, ML Engineer, Data Scientist, AI Researcher, Business Intelligence Developer, and Data Engineer.
